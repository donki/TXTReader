# Application Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

The application respects the privacy of its users. This privacy policy explains how data is handled when using the application.

## Data Collection

The application does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The application may access text files selected by the user solely to allow them to be read within the application. These files remain on the user's device and are not sent to any external server.

## Use of Information

Any information or files opened in the application are used exclusively to display their content to the user within the application.

## Data Sharing

The application does not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

The application does not use external analytics, advertising, or user-tracking services.

## Security

The application is designed to operate locally on the device. Even so, users are advised to keep their device up to date and to protect their personal files.

## Children's Privacy

The application is not specifically directed at children and does not collect personal information from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this same page with a new update date.
